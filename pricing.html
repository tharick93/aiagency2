What happens if I exceed my monthly GPU hours?

Excess hours are billed at a flat rate of $0.85 per hour on Starter and Professional plans. Enterprise plans feature unrestricted compute limits and do not incur overflow rates.

Can I change plans mid-billing period?

Yes. When upgrading, remaining credits are automatically calculated and subtracted from your next invoice. Downgrades take effect at the start of your subsequent billing cycle.
